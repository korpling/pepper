Detailed Description

Status of a job in Pepper, the status could be one of the following:

  • NOT_STARTED, if the job was not started
  • IN_PROGRESS, if the job is running
  • ENDED, if the job ended successful
  • ENDED_WITH_ERRORS, if the job ended with errors
